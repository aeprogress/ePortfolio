Professionalism

Definition

To be professional is to perform effectively and responsibly when facing a professional task. That includes both local and international context. To be able to be professional, a strong emphasis should be put toward ethics. Also, diversity and negotiation skills for a modern work environment are important.

Assignment 1: Professional Preparation Readiness

Introduction

In this assignment the students were asked to take couple of personality tests and asked to reflect on the results of these tests. This artefact gave me a further understanding of my personality and an insight to why I behave certain ways, therefore, I picked this assignment to represent the professionalism competency. The skills used to develop this artefact were critical thinking, and self-understanding.

Reflection

This assignment taught me a lot about myself; for example, learned that I lean towards having more left-brain tendencies, which means I am more mathematical and logical. I also learned that understanding oneself is a crustal part of professional development. Moreover, I learned that all my action and interaction have a deeper explanation that seeds in my personality and bringing that to the surface by analysing it will help me further become a better part of society. To improve this assignment further I would dig deeper into my personality to have more understanding of who I am and why I behave the way I do. When doing similar assignment, I suggest not to take the final results of the tests as an absolute truth; instead, investigate and reflect on your answers.

Assignment 2: Social Equality (presentation)

Introduction

The students were asked to pick a topic from the text book, search about it, and present it to the class. This assignment was done in groups. I picked this assignment to represent the professionalism because I think the topic of the presentation “Social Equality” is an important part of professional development. By presenting this topic, I developed a better presentation skill, and teamwork skills.

Reflection

From this assignment I learned the different types of equalities and how they can affect the professional development of various sectors of society. Furthermore, I learned that equality of opportunity is one of the most important equality, because without it, one can not develop professionally. To had had done this assignment better, I would read more about the different types of equalities, including the work environment equality. To whom interested in similar assignment, I suggest learning about more examples of such topic, further investigate your equality rights.

Assignment 3: Data Structure assignment II (Queues)

Introduction

For this assignment students were asked to solve a problem using the knowledge they have in Java programming and what they just learned in data structure (Queues). The problem was about a line of customers in a bank, in which, a new customer arrives every four minutes, and every customer will take seven minutes to be served. We had to write a program that keeps track of this queue. This assignment relates to professionalism because, it taught me how to achieve a task effectively and responsibly. I chose this assignment in the professionalism category to show case a professional task related to my major (IT), and the way I approached the problem.

Reflection

Going through this assignment made me think harder about the nature of it. I had to imagen how a queue actually works and how to keep track of it. Timing was important in this problem, as I had to keep track of arrival time, time of departure, how many customers are in the line, and who is being served in the moment. To do all that, I went back to the skills I learned in earlier course (Computer Since II). For example, how to manipulate loops in such way to be effective for this problem. I also learned from this assignment that sometimes the best solutions are not the most complicated. I believe this assignment has brought me a step forward towards professionalism. If I had another shot at this assignment, I would consider visualizing it more, so that the user would have a better interaction with the program. To whom interested in such problem, I suggest trying to imagen the problem before start solving it. Once you fully understood how every part of the queue works, start working on the technical programing side.
