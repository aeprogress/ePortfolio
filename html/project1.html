Communication

Definition

Communication is the sharing of meaningful concurrent ideas. To have the competency of communication is to be able to communicate in one or more languages in professional and social situations. Communication can be written, oral, or nonverbal.

Assignment 1: Letter of complaint

Introduction

In this assignment the students were asked to group in pares of two, and one of the group members was asked to take the rowel of a manager of some sort, and the other member to be either a costumer or an employee with a complaint. The purpose of this assignment is to maintain a professional communication via emails. The skills were used in this assignment were group work, and professional writing.

Reflection

I learned from this assignment how to professionally communicate with other employee while maintaining an advantage. Dealing with an angry employer or employee can go south very fast, unless, some professional communication skills were used. These skills include; using proper language, staying away from insulting the receiver, keeping focused on the subject matter. To do this assignment better I would support my arguments with more avoidances, such as providing prove of hard work and such. To whom interested in doing similar assignment I advise you to go throw the points mentioned in each email with your group member, and think of ways to write it better. By doing so, the final draft of the assignment will be much clearer and professional.

This artefact improved my oral communication skills, there for, I picked it to represent the communication competency. The skills were used to develop this artefact were research skills, writing skills, and personation skills.

I learned many thingy things from this assignment. Most importantly I learned how to properly search about a topic that I had no background about. As a result, I developed new searching skills in which I stated to distinguish between what is key point and should be included in my personation and which was not. Assignment 3: Coding Java with NetBeans: Starter’s Manual

Introduction

For this assignment students were divided into groups of three members. We were asked to choose a subject to create a technical manual. All members of my group were in the computer science collage. so, we picked a topic that we can all relate to. NetBeans is a program that compiles different types of programing languages. After doing the necessary research, we started writing and designing the manual. This assignment relates to communication competency; as it taught me how to communicate my ideas with my group members, and how to write and design a technical manual.

Reflection

Writing a manual for the first time was an interesting experience. One of the things I learned doing this assignment is how to be practical and precise while brief. I also learned how to find critical points and organize them in chronological order. When communicating one has to consider the audience. By doing so, we were able to make a clear and easy to read manual. Working in a team for this assignment was very productive. Everyone shared what they think the manual should look like and what are the most important steps that should be mentioned in it. This teamwork taught me how to explain what I was thinking and how to compromise my idea for a better one. If I had to do a manual again, I would test my work by consulting expert individuals on the manual subject. To whom interested on doing similar assignment, I suggest looking into reference models and understanding which models would fit your manual topic, then make your won.
