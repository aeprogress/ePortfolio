What are they?

Prince Mohammed Bin Fahad university (PMU) is proud to include six competencies in their core course. These competencies include, communication, critical thinking, leadership, teamwork, professionalism, and technology. Each one of these courses is meant to prepare the students for their work life. The students are given verity of individual and group assignments to practice and apply these competencies.
Taking these courses, I have noticed huge benefits interim of the way I think and behave in certain situation. In a world advancing dramatically, I advise anyone who wants to have a professional and thoughtful life to study these competencies.
